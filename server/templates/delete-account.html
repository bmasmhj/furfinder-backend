Account & Data Deletion

Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request the deletion of your The Fur Finder account and all associated data at any time, in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account details (email, display name, phone number)
  • All pet reports you have created
  • All registered pet profiles
  • Your comments on pet reports
  • Biometric scan data
  • Notification history
  • Referral and ambassador data
  • Likes, blocks, and content reports
  • Subscription and consent records

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: In the App

  1. Open The Fur Finder and log in
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to Account
  4. Tap Delete My Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Email Request

  1. Send an email to the address below
  2. Include the email address associated with your account
  3. We will process your request within 48 hours
📧 Request Account Deletion via Email

Data Retention

Once your account is deleted:

  • All personal data is immediately removed from our active database
  • Backups containing your data are purged within 30 days
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data may be retained
